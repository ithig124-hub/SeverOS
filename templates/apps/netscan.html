ℹ️ How NetScan Works

NetScan reads the Linux ARP neighbor table (ip neighbor show) to list devices that have recently communicated on your local network. REACHABLE means the device responded recently. STALE means it hasn't responded but was seen before. Run this on your Raspberry Pi / hotspot to see all connected clients.
